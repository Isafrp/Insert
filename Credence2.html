CREDENCE: Collaborative Network for Excellence in Education and Research on Reliable and Secure Distributed Systems.

Leobino Sampaio

Last updated on September 1, 2020

CREDENCE addresses all aspects related to Blockchain as well as distributed systems more broadly, leveraging the expertise of partners in publish/subscribe mechanisms, high-performance computing, and socio-technical aspects. In particular, the proposed collaboration will strengthen the BBChain project by advancing the state of the art in the design, evaluation, and validation of blockchain infrastructures that are sensitive to exploits, including those used for cryptocurrencies, biometric-based document storage, and high-volume data streams requiring integrity protection.
