QUALISALVADOR

Elisabete Santos and Leobino Nascimento Sampaio

Status: Ongoing

The Urban Environment Quality Project of Salvador, QUALISalvador, focuses on studying the urban and environmental reality of Salvador. Its general objective is to produce and disseminate knowledge about this reality at the intra-urban scale.
