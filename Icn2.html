Human and Context Awareness as Support for Mobility in Information-Centric Networks.

Leobino Sampaio, Rafael Costa

Last updated on September 1, 2020

The project consists of an international cooperation proposal whose main objective is to identify new strategies that contribute to data offloading in information-centric mobile networks.

Partners:

  • Federal University of Bahia (UFBA)
  • National Institute for Research in Computer Science and Automation (INRIA).
