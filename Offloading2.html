Support for Data Offloading in Wireless Mobile Networks

Leobino Nascimento Sampaio, Rafael Lima Costa, Adriana Viriato Ribeiro, Nilton Flávio Sousa Seixas, Eliseu Silva Torres, Aline Carneiro Viana

Last updated on September 1, 2020

The project consists of an international cooperation proposal whose main objective is to identify new strategies that contribute to data offloading in mobile networks, considering users' contextual information and the use of concepts from RCC (Content-Centric Networks) and RCU (User-Centric Networks). These approaches allow new network characteristics to be leveraged for data offloading. To achieve this goal, a partnership between INRIA (Institut National de Recherche en Informatique et en Automatique) and the Federal University of Bahia (UFBA) has been established to combine the experiences and efforts already pursued by researchers from both institutions. As a result of this research, the following outcomes are expected: (1) Identify contextual information that can contribute to data offloading in RCC and RCU scenarios; (2) Develop a framework for building environments that support RCC and RCU; (3) Create an intelligent and proactive storage strategy in RCC device caches to enable data offloading; (4) Develop an opportunistic communication strategy in RCU to improve data offloading efficiency.
