Other Initiatives

Collaboration with Prof. Fabio Verdi, from the Federal University of São Carlos (UFSCar), which resulted in the work “Study on characteristics of computer network administrators in Brazil for identification and creation of personas”, presented in the main track of the 2017 edition of the Brazilian Symposium on Computer Networks and Distributed Systems (SBRC).

Organization of the ACM SIGCOMM 2016 edition, held in Florianópolis in August 2016. SIGCOMM is one of the world's leading computing events and in 2016 it was held for the first time in Latin America.

Participation in the Global Experimentation for Future Internet (GEFI) Workshop, a group of researchers from the European Union, United States, Brazil, Japan, and South Korea, to define the directions of global collaboration in the Future Internet. New network architectures, services and applications, federations, cloud computing, Internet of Things, and wireless networks are some of the fields of study.
