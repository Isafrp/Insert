Humans in the Communication Cycle Through Named Data Networks

Leobino Sampaio

This project investigates how to explore the properties of the NDN architecture in developing human-aware networks in mobile edge computing scenarios by evaluating three key aspects: (i) mobility, (ii) security, and (iii) distributed applications.
