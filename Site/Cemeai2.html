CeMEAI (USP/UFBA)

Francisco Louzada Neto / Deputy Coordinator: Andre Carlos Ponce de Leon Ferreira de Carvalho

**MISSION:** Provide resources and mechanisms to connect scientists, engineers, mathematicians, and computing specialists to solve challenging industrial problems by applying mathematical sciences techniques in a collaborative and engaging environment. Train high-level human resources and develop new transformative mathematical techniques while exploring their applications for the benefit of society. **VISION:** To be a leader in the application of Mathematical Sciences techniques in the industry in the State of São Paulo, fostering innovations and interdisciplinary collaborations, increasing its impact on the productive sector, and serving as a bridge between high-level mathematics and problems of scientific and technological importance. To be a Center of Excellence in strategic areas for the country's technological development, expanding and strengthening the talent base in Mathematical Sciences with skills for interdisciplinary applications.
