BAMBU: A Metropolitan Innovation Platform to Promote Research on the Future Internet in the City of Salvador.

Leobino Sampaio, Allan Freitas, Ítalo Valcy Brito, André Luiz Madureira, Adriana Viriato Ribeiro

Last updated on September 1, 2020

The main objective is to design and implement a metropolitan innovation platform to promote practical research on the Future Internet in the city of Salvador, Bahia.

Partners:

  • Federal University of Bahia (UFBA)
  • Federal Institute of Education, Science, and Technology of Bahia (IFBA)
  • Gonçalo Moniz Research Center – Bahia (Fiocruz-Ba)
  • National Education and Research Network (RNP)
  • Federal University of Espírito Santo (UFES)
  • National Laboratory for Scientific Computing (LNCC)
  • Florida International University (FIU)
  • Philips Research North America
