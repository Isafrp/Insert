GT HackInSDN

Leobino Nascimento Sampaio - Coordenador / Italo Valcy da Silva Brito - Integrante / Allan Edgard Silva Freitas - Integrante.

From the experience gained with SDN-IPS, the proposal aims to develop the HackInSDN architecture, which seeks to expand the capacity-building in cybersecurity topics in testbed environments. It is a toolset that will offer a more robust and comprehensive environment for training in advanced security topics through network programmability. HackInSDN will cover topics beyond intrusion detection systems, incorporating other features such as novelty and anomaly detection mechanisms supported by Artificial Intelligence, dynamic attack containment and filtering mechanisms, tools for simulating attacks, threat intelligence databases, among others. Just like the previous development, HackInSDN is designed to be operated in the distributed testbed environment of RNP, benefiting the RNP system with a cybersecurity training service that will adopt its testbed infrastructure..
