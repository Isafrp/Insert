An Investigation into the Maintainability of Machine Learning Systems

Eduardo Santana de Almeida

This research project has three main objectives. First, we aim to investigate the differences between ML and conventional systems. Next, our goal is to propose metrics and quality indicators for ML system design. Finally, we aim to recommend solutions to improve design quality and code refactoring to ensure system maintainability.
