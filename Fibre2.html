Experimentation in the Future of the Internet between Brazil and Europe (FIBRE-BR)

Jose A. Suruagy Monteiro, Leobino Nascimento Sampaio, and Joberto S. B. Martins.

Status: Completed

The main objective of this project is to create a common space between the EU and Brazil for experimental research on the Future Internet (FI) in network infrastructure and distributed applications. Currently, facilities of this nature are already being operated or are under construction following similar projects by partners on both sides of the Atlantic Ocean. It is expected that such a space will enable and encourage closer and more comprehensive bilateral cooperation in FI research and experimentation, as well as strengthen the participation of both communities in increasingly important global collaborations in this critical area of network research and development.
